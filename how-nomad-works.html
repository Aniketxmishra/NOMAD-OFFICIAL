How NOMAD Works

Welcome to NOMAD! Our platform connects people for shared adventures, quests, and trips while also providing companionship for the elderly. Here’s how it works:

1. Sign Up

Create an account on NOMAD to get started. You can sign up using your email or social media accounts.

2. Create a Profile

Complete your profile with information about your interests, hobbies, and what kind of adventures you’re looking for. You can also specify if you are interested in providing companionship for the elderly.

3. Find Adventures

Browse through a list of available adventures, quests, and trips. You can filter by location, type of activity, and dates.

4. Join or Create an Adventure

Join an existing adventure or create your own. If you create your own, you can invite others to join you.

5. Connect with Others

Communicate with other members through our messaging system to plan your adventure. For those providing companionship, you can connect with elderly individuals in need of company.

6. Enjoy Your Adventure

Embark on your adventure, make new friends, and create unforgettable experiences!

7. Share Your Experience

After your adventure, share your stories and photos with the NOMAD community. Your experiences can inspire others to join or create their own adventures.
